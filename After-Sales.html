Cirrus Calibration Services

To illustrate Cirrus' calibration process, when an Optimus sound level meter arrives at our lab, we meticulously assess its microphone capsule using our reference electrostatic calibration system. This rigorous evaluation covers a wide frequency range, various sound levels, and the microphone's absolute sensitivity. Only if the capsule passes these tests is it approved for use with the sound level meter.
Subsequently, the entire instrument undergoes a series of tests mirroring those conducted during its initial production. These tests assess parameters like frequency and time weightings accuracy and linearity across different levels. This ensures consistent accuracy for both environmental and occupational noise measurements.
Only after successfully passing these meticulous assessments, accompanied by a certificate of calibration, do we consider the instrument calibrated and ready for use. This detailed process is essential to ensure the reliability and accuracy of your noise measurement instruments.
